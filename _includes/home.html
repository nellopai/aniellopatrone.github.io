About me

I am an experienced scientist in the area of computer vision and deep learning. I have several years of experience in deployment of AI software, project management, and lateral leadership. I empower people with insights from data.

I earned a PhD in computer science with a focus on computer vision, an MSc and a BSc in computer science. I have several years of professional research experience in mathematical image and data analysis.
